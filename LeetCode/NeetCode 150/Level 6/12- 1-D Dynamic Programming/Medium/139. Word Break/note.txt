The used Algorithmic Paradigm:
  Dynamic programming.
  Time And Space Complexity:
    My Optimal Solution And Neetcode Solution:
      Time Complexity value is O(n * m * t).
      Space Complexity value is O(n).
        `n` represents the length of the word we are searching for.
        `m` represents the number of the words in our dictionary.
        `t` represents the longest dictionary word length.
